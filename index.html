Highlights
  • Boss Rush Design — one major handcrafted encounter.
  • Pixel 2D Platforming — jump, move, dodge, attack.
  • Fire vs. Ice Theme — lore-inspired elemental contrast.
  • Hackathon Build — created from scratch in a few weeks.

Overview

Winter Tale is a 2D pixel boss challenge developed from zero during a month-long hackathon. The player controls a young girl with fire abilities to defeat a bear capable of ice magic.

Team

Bintao Chi: core logic, engine foundation, asset preparation.
Augustus Lin: supporting development,html Presentation.

Design Intent

Our goal was to create a fully original, small-scope action experience inspired by classic winter fairy-tale themes, focusing on a single carefully tuned boss encounter.

Core Features

A compact 2D boss combat experience with platforming fundamentals.

Fire Abilities

Player attacks and mechanics themed around warmth and flame.

Ice Boss

A bear capable of ranged and area-based ice magic attacks.

Precise Controls

Jumping, movement, dodging, timing-based combat.

Pixel Art Style

A retro winter aesthetic inspired by classic SNES-era visuals.

From-Scratch Build

Developed from zero in a four-week hackathon timeframe.

FAQ

Common questions about the Winter Tale project.

Is this a full game?

No. It is a small-scope hackathon prototype focusing on one boss.

Why a fire girl vs an ice bear?

We wanted a fairy-tale winter theme with strong elemental contrast.

What tools were used?

Custom codebase, pixel references, and partially AI-generated art.

Will this be expanded?

Possibly in the future, depending on team schedule and interest.
