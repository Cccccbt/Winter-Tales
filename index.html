What to expect

Hybrid-friendly hacking with workshops, office hours, and cozy lounge spaces. Submit from anywhere or join us in person for warm cocoa, live music, and late-night debugging.

Who should join

Developers, designers, narrative writers, sound artists, and anyone curious about building interactive experiences. Newcomers welcome—mentors will help you ship.

How judging works

Teams demo on Sunday to a panel of industry judges. Projects are scored on creativity, execution, accessibility, and narrative depth.

FAQ

Answers to common questions before you pack your laptop.

Do I need a team before registering?

No. We run mixers and online channels to help you meet collaborators. Solo registrations are welcome.

Is travel support available?

Yes. Limited stipends are available for students and first-time attendees. Apply during registration.

Can I use existing code?

Starting frameworks are fine. New features and storytelling must be created during the event.

How are winners announced?

Finalists demo on Sunday afternoon. Winners are celebrated during the closing ceremony and online stream.
